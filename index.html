US Food Insecurity During the COVID-19 Pandemic

The ongoing COVID-19 pandemic has a great impact on people's lives, and has brought a lot of underlying socioeconomic issues to light. Food insecurity issue is one of them that we will be looking at today.

Food is a basic human need, and having a consistent access to enough food to sustain a healthy life is absolutely essential.

The United States Census Bureau's Pulse Survey includes a section of Food Sufficiency for Households. We'll look at the dataset and see how different demographics experience food insecurity issue differently throughout the pandemic.
